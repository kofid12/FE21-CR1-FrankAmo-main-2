Spaghetti Bolognese

This classic spaghetti Bolognese Recipe is rich , hearty and perfect for a comforting dinner .Here is how you can make it

    • 500g ground beef
    • 1 chopped onion
    • 2 minced garlic cloves
    • 1 can 400g crushed tomatoes
    • 1/2 cup tomato paste
    • 1 cup beef broth
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • salt and pepper to taste
    • 300 g spaghetti

  • INSTRUCTIONS

    1. In a large skillet,brown the ground beef over medium heat
    2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic ,cook until softened
    3. Stir in crushed tomatoes ,tomaton paste ,beef broth,oregano,salt and pepper .Simmer for 20-30 minutes
    4. Cook the spaghetti according to package instruction
    5. serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti
